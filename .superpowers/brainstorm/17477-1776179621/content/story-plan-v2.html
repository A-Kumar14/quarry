Gaza ceasefire talks collapse after prisoner swap fails
What happened
Qatari-mediated negotiations between Israel and Hamas broke down on Tuesday after both sides failed to reach agreement on a prisoner exchange. Israeli negotiators left Doha without a deal. Aid convoys have remained blocked at the Kerem Shalom crossing since Monday.
Background
This is the third collapse of ceasefire talks in six weeks. The previous rounds broke down over disagreements on the number of hostages and the duration of a sustained pause in fighting.

LocationDoha, Qatar — negotiations; Gaza Strip — fighting
